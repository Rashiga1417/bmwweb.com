About BMW

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) is a German multinational company that produces luxury vehicles and motorcycles. Founded in 1916, BMW has a long history of innovation in automotive engineering. The company is known for its high-performance cars, precision engineering, and luxury design. BMW also leads in electric vehicle technology with its i-Series models. The brand's philosophy revolves around driving pleasure, technological advancement, and sustainability.
